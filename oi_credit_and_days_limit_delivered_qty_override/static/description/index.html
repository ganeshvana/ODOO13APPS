Credit Limit Based on Value and Days Limit - Delivered Quantity

Steps to Enable Credit Limit Value and Override Option

Step 1:

Option to enable credit limit and to override credit limit is available in Settings-->Users Menu.

Step 2:

Enable credit limit applicable field and value can be mentioned in credit limit field available in partner master(Sales Menu--Orders-->Customers).

Step 3:

choose sales menu-->orders-->create order and make sure products chosen contains delivered quantities as invoicing policy in product master.

Step 4:

Warning is raised on confirmation when order value exceeds the limit defined and the order gets confirmed.

Step 5:

On validating delivery order, warning is raised when credit limit value exceeds the limit defined in customer master.

Step 6:

Option to override credit limit is available in delivery order-->Additional Info tab.

Step 7:

override credit limit on enabling allows to validate delivery by overriding the limit defined.

Steps to Enable Days Limit

Step 1:

Option to enable Days Limit is available in Settings-->Users Menu.

Step 2:

Enable Days Expiry Applicable field and value can be mentioned in Days Limit field available in partner master(Sales Menu--Orders-->Customers).

Step 3:

choose sales menu-->orders-->create order and make sure products chosen contains delivered quantities as invoicing policy in product master-->Confirm Order-->Create Invoice

Step 4:

Warning is raised on validating invoice when days value exceeds the limit defined in partner master.
