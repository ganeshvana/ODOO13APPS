Payment Adjustment Amount Against Invoice

A Company receives or does makes a payment to partner for a single transactions or for multiple transactions. It will be much easier to track a payment against single bills/invoice whereas in if its multiple, it would be difficult to track. Hence to make it simple, we (Oodu) have developed this module to view your adjusted bills/invoices against the total amount paid or received.

This module works for Invoice & vendor bills too. Just install the module, make a bulk payment and adjust the payment for invoice/bill wise. Once you have adjusted the payment, take a print out of Payment receipt for the payment you have made. The payment print out will show you, the Actual Paid Amount (additional column) against each invoice/bills adjusted.

Choose Accounting-->Vendors-->Payments-->Payment Receipt Report. Additional column actual paid amount appears in the report.

Step 1:

Install the module for payment receipt print.

Step 2:

Goto Vendor Payments and print payment receipt.

Additional column actual amount paid can be viewed.

This column fetches actual amount adjusted in the payment against invoice.
