This module, on installation hides the sales unit price, tax and subtotal in sale order form

Step 1:

Choose sales Menu.

Step 2:

Click on NEW QUOTATION button for creating quotation.

Step 3:

Select partner and choose order line products. Price, Tax & subtotal is not visible

Step 4:

Goto --> Settings --> Users & Companies --> Users --> sale pricelist visible in the sale order boolean field is available.

Step 5:

Now, enable the sale pricelist visible in the sale order.

Step 6:

Fields are visible in the sale order form.
